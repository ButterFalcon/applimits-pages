Getting Started

What is ApLimit?

ApLimit is an iOS app designed to help you manage your smartphone usage and improve focus. You can set time limits for specific apps or categories, block apps during bedtime, and use the Pomodoro timer to stay concentrated on your work.

Key Features:

  • Schedule Management: Set time limits by day and time period
  • Downtime: Block apps during specific hours like bedtime
  • Focus Timer: Use the Pomodoro technique for concentration
  • Hard Limitation: A mode that prevents immediate removal of restrictions
How do I get started with ApLimit?

Step 1: Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to grant Screen Time permissions.

Step 2: Tap the "+" button on the home screen to create a new schedule.

Step 3: Select the apps or categories you want to limit.

Step 4: Set the days and time limits (you can also use presets).

Step 5: Tap "Save" to complete the setup.

What's the difference between Schedule and Downtime?

Schedule: Sets daily usage time limits for specific apps or categories on selected days. For example, "Limit social media to 30 minutes per day on weekdays."

Downtime: Completely blocks selected apps or categories during specific time periods (e.g., 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM). Perfect for managing bedtime.

What can I do with the free version?

The free version includes:

  • Schedules: Up to 1 schedule
  • App Selection: Up to 2 apps or categories
  • Downtime: Unlimited settings
  • Basic schedule management features

If you need more schedules or want to manage more apps, consider upgrading to the Pro plan.

Features

What is Hard Limitation?

Hard Limitation is a powerful restriction feature that, once enabled, cannot be disabled until the next day. It's perfect for strengthening your self-control.

Features:

  • Once enabled, settings cannot be changed or deleted until the end of the day
  • Available for Schedules, Focus Timer, and Downtime
  • This is a Pro plan exclusive feature

Important: Please review your settings carefully before enabling Hard Limitation, as you cannot undo it once activated.

How do I use the Focus Timer?

Focus Timer uses the Pomodoro technique to enhance concentration (Pro plan exclusive).

How to use:

  1. Open the Focus Timer tab
  2. Select the apps you want to restrict
  3. Set the timer duration (15 min, 30 min, 1 hour, or custom)
  4. Enable Hard Limitation if desired
  5. Tap "Start"

When the timer ends, you'll receive a notification and your configured break time will begin.

What does "Exclude Today" mean?

The "Exclude Today" feature allows you to delay the start of a schedule until tomorrow.

Use cases:

  • You need to use an app today for special reasons but want restrictions from tomorrow
  • You want to prepare schedules in advance

To re-enable the schedule for today, use "Reset All Schedules" in the settings screen.

Can I restrict by category instead of individual apps?

Yes, you can. The app selection screen allows you to choose entire categories in addition to individual apps.

Available category examples:

  • Social Networking
  • Entertainment
  • Games
  • And many more

When you select a category, all apps within that category will be automatically restricted.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription through your iPhone settings:

  1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top of the screen
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "ApLimit"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

After canceling, you'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

What happens after the 7-day free trial?

Troubleshooting

My restrictions aren't working

If your restrictions aren't working, please check the following:

1. Verify Screen Time permissions

  • Go to iPhone "Settings" → "Screen Time" → "ApLimit" and verify permissions are enabled

2. Check schedule settings

  • Are the target days enabled?
  • Are the time limits set correctly?
  • Is "Exclude Today" enabled?

3. Try resetting schedules

  • Tap "Reset All Schedules" in the settings screen

If the problem persists, please contact us through the contact form.

How do I reset my schedules?

If your schedules aren't working properly or you want to reset everything:

  1. Open the Settings screen
  2. Tap "Reset All Schedules"
  3. Tap "OK" in the confirmation dialog

Note: This operation will re-apply all schedules and Downtime settings. Your existing schedule configurations will be preserved, but the iOS-side restrictions will be re-registered.

I can't edit or delete a schedule

Main reasons you can't edit or delete a schedule:

Hard Limitation is enabled

  • When Hard Limitation is enabled, you cannot make changes until the end of the day
  • You'll be able to edit or delete the schedule the next day

Focus Timer is running

  • You need to wait until the timer ends

These are intentional features designed to strengthen self-control.

Can I prevent ApLimit from being deleted?

You can use iOS Screen Time features to prevent deletion of ApLimit itself:

  1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone
  2. Tap "Screen Time"
  3. Tap "Content & Privacy Restrictions"
  4. Tap "iTunes & App Store Purchases"
  5. Set "Deleting Apps" to "Don't Allow"

Setting a Screen Time passcode will provide even stronger protection.

Privacy & Security

What permissions does ApLimit need?

ApLimit requires the following permissions:

Screen Time permission: Required

  • Necessary to restrict app usage time
  • Required for all ApLimit features to function

Notification permission: Optional

  • Necessary to receive Focus Timer completion notifications
  • The app will work without this permission, but you won't receive timer notifications
How does ApLimit handle my data?

ApLimit prioritizes your privacy:

  • All data is stored on your device
  • Schedule settings and app usage information are not sent externally
  • Subscription information is managed by Apple
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
